Local Bitcoins

LocalBitcoins is like a Craigslist for Bitcoin sales and purchases. You can use LocalBitcoins to buy and sell Bitcoin via cash, check, bank deposit, and other payment methods. All Bitcoin is held in escrow and the company offers dispute resolution in case something goes wrong.

LocalBitcoins has a great step-by-step guide on using their site Here

Buying and selling operates the same way. Below, we discuss how to buy:

  1. Register your new account Here

  2. Fill in the search box with the amount you want to buy in your currency, your location, and choose a payment method.

    After you hit Search a list of advertisements will appear

  3. Choose an advertisement with over 50 trades and a reputation score above 90%

  4. The response time indicator shows if a trader replies within five minutes (green), within 30 minutes (yellow) or slower than 30 minutes (grey).

  5. Note the minimum and maximum limits along with the price of Bitcoin quoted by each seller.

  6. You can click Buy to view more information about an advertisement and terms of the trade. If you do not like the terms of the trade, you can go back to the previous page and look at other sellers.

  7. Input how much you want to buy.

  8. Enter a message for the seller and click Send trade request button to the start the trade. Be sure you're ready to pay when clicking the button. Payment windows vary depending on the type of payment.

  9. Click the Mark payment complete button after you have paid the seller. Your Bitcoin will be released from escrow and available in your LocalBitcoins wallet once the seller verifies your payment.

To access your LocalBitcoins exchange wallet:

  1. Click Wallet:

  2. To send Bitcoin out of this wallet simply choose Send Bitcoins. Input the receiving address and the amount, and follow the prompts to send.

  3. Choose Receive Bitcoins to obtain your LocalBitcoins Exchange wallet address so you may receive Bitcoin.
