Copay - Backup Wallet

Your Copay wallet should serve as one of your “cold” wallets. You should use it for holding large amounts of bitcoin. Although Copay gives you an option to buy or sell Bitcoin directly from your wallet, our platform has temporarily disabled this feature to ensure security.

Wallet Setup

  1. Open your Copay wallet by finding the Copy logo in your left-side menu or by searching “Copay” in your search bar.

  2. Go through the brief introduction starting with “Get Started” until you get to the option to “Backup Wallet”. Do not skip this step!

  3. Write down the phase they provide somewhere safe. Inca Digital Securities does not save your backup phrase. If you lose your Copay wallet and you don’t have your backup phrase, you will lose any Bitcoin stored in that wallet.

Receiving Bitcoin

  1. To receive Bitcoin in this wallet, simply click the "Receive" icon on the bottom toolbar.

  2. Click the QR code. This will copy your receiving address to your clipboard. Paste that receiving address into your exchange wallet and click send. It will take around 10 minutes for you to receive your funds.

Sending Bitcoin

  1. To send funds out of this wallet, simply click the "Send" icon on the bottom toolbar.

  2. Copy and paste the wallet address where you want to send the funds under “Recipient”.


  3. Enter the amount you want to send and push the green arrow to send it off.
